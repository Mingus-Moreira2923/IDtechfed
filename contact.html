Frequently Asked Questions

How can my startup join the Idaho Tech Federation?

To join the Idaho Tech Federation, you'll need to submit an application through our website. Once submitted, our team will review your application and give you a call if accepted.

What resources does the Federation provide for startups?

The Federation provides free marketing. But we are hoping to expand to networking events in the future.

Are there membership fees to join?

No,we are a non profit organization. Donations would be appreciated.

How can I volunteer or mentor with the Federation?

We're not taking volunteers currently. But we will let people know through our socials, when we are. In the mean time feel free to tell people/post about us.
